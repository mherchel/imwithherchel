Frequently Asked Questions

Why are you running for president?

Our current major party nominees are disgusting. One is borderline criminal, and the other is absolutely unstable. I can do better. We can do better.

I want to take special interests away from Washington. I want to invest in the future. This includes environment, education, and the economy.

How did this idea originate?

The initial idea started out as a joke. The thinking was the current nominees are horrible, and I can do better. From there, I thought to myself, “Why can’t I have a platform?”. It took off from there.

Who’s your favorite president?

Teddy Roosevelt by far. He broke up big trusts, and was not afraid to go against the system.
